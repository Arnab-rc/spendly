Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using Spendly. This includes expense transactions, categories, descriptions, dates, and amounts that you enter to track your personal finances.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the purpose of helping you track and understand your spending patterns. Your data is used to generate summaries, category breakdowns, and time-based filters within your account.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data is stored securely and is accessible only through your authenticated account. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, though no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal financial data to outside parties. Your expense information remains private to your account unless you choose to export or share it.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your expense data at any time through your account settings. You may also request a complete export of all your stored information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com
